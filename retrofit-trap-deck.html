the redesign

There is no monthly review.

Every market's true state is live and drill-downable. The moment something material moves, the system surfaces it — with a recommended call — to whoever owns that lever, when it moves, not on a calendar.

a human still owns the decision
and it's worse than waste

Every tier doesn't just relay. It polishes.

By the time the story climbs from a local floor to the global boardroom, it's been curated, softened, and aged by weeks. The cascade doesn't just cost time — it launders the truth on the way up.
